Chinese-Style Spicy Chicken with Ginger and Onion 

Ingredients: 
- 2 chicken breasts, cut into cubes
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 tablespoons of freshly grated ginger
- 2 tablespoons of chili sauce

Instructions:

1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add the chicken cubes. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through.
2. Add the diced onion and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the onion is softened.
3. Add the grated ginger and chili sauce and stir to combine. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce is thickened and the chicken is fully cooked.
4. Serve the Chinese-style spicy chicken with ginger and onion over steamed white rice. Enjoy!
