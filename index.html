Recipe Title: Spicy Beef with Peppers

Ingredients:

- 2 lb. beef chuck, cubed
- 1/4 cup vegetable oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons fresh ginger, minced
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
- 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 2 bell peppers, chopped
- 1/2 cup beef broth
- 2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
- 2 tablespoons water
- 2 tablespoons Hoisin sauce
- 2 teaspoons black pepper
- 2 tablespoons chopped scallions
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions:

1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
2. Add cubed beef and cook until browned, stirring often.
3. Add garlic, ginger, cornstarch, sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil, and red pepper flakes. Stir to combine.
4. Add bell peppers, beef broth, rice wine vinegar, water, Hoisin sauce, and black pepper. Stir to combine.
