Recipe Title: Spicy Ginger Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients:
- 2 chicken breasts, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons freshly grated ginger
- 2 green onions, sliced
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons spicy oil

Instructions:
1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add the spicy oil.
2. Add the diced chicken and cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through.
3. Add the garlic, ginger, and green onions and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
4. Add the soy sauce and stir to combine.
5. Cook for an additional 2 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are tender and the chicken is cooked through.
6. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
