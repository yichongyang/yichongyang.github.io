Recipe Title: Spicy Mapo Tofu

Ingredients:
- 10 oz firm tofu, cubed
- 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons ginger, minced
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 1 teaspoon Sichuan peppercorns, ground
- 1 teaspoon chili paste
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons black bean paste
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions:

1. Heat 2 tablespoons of the vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat.

2. Add the garlic, ginger and onion and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.

3. Add the Sichuan peppercorns, chili paste, soy sauce, black bean paste, and sugar and stir to combine.

4. Add the chicken broth and bring to a simmer.

5. Add the cubed tofu and stir to combine. Reduce the heat to low and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

6. Heat the remaining 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil in a separate small skillet over medium-high heat.

7. Add the sesame oil
