

Recipe Title: Spicy Szechuan Mapo Tofu 

Ingredients: 
- 1 block of firm tofu, cut into cubes
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon of freshly grated ginger
- 2 tablespoons of doubanjiang (spicy fermented bean paste)
- 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
- 1 teaspoon of sugar
- ï¿½ teaspoon of Szechuan peppercorns, toasted and ground
- 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons of chili oil
- 2 tablespoons of water
- 1 scallion, sliced
- 1 teaspoon of toasted sesame oil

Instructions: 
1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add the vegetable oil.

2. Once the oil is hot, add the garlic and ginger. Saute for 30 seconds until fragrant.

3. Add the doubanjiang and stir for a few seconds until it is combined with the garlic and ginger.

4. Add the tofu cubes and stir until they are coated in the sauce.

5. Add the soy sauce, sugar, and Szechuan peppercorns. Stir until everything is combined.

6. Add the chili oil, water.
