

Recipe Title: Spicy Sichuan-Style Mapo Tofu

Ingredients:

-14 oz firm tofu, drained and cubed
-1 tablespoon vegetable oil 
-2 cloves garlic, minced
-1 teaspoon freshly grated ginger
-2 tablespoons Sichuan peppercorns, coarsely ground
-2 tablespoons chili bean paste
-1/4 cup chicken broth
-1/4 cup soy sauce
-2 tablespoons rice wine
-2 tablespoons cornstarch
-1 teaspoon white sugar
-2 green onions, thinly sliced
-1 tablespoon sesame oil

Instructions:

1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
2. Add the garlic, ginger, and Sichuan peppercorns to the pan and cook until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
3. Add the chili bean paste and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
4. Add the chicken broth, soy sauce, rice wine, cornstarch, sugar, and tofu cubes to the pan.
5. Bring the mixture to a boil and then reduce the heat to low. Simmer the mixture for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
6. Remove the pan from the heat and stir in the green onions and sesame oil.
7. Serve the Mapo Tofu hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
